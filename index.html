Who this is for

If you are a follower of Christ and a business owner, you've likely felt the tension of making decisions that honor the Lord while increasing growth and profitability — all while trying to maintain a healthy rhythm for work, family, friends, and community. Many of us carry these responsibilities feeling alone, without other executives around who truly understand.

Flatirons wants to help!

What to expect

We'll spend time talking about the intersection of faith and work — what some call the theology of work — and how what we believe about our work shapes how we think about Jesus in everyday decisions.

This won't be a presentation-heavy event. It's a chance to have authentic conversations with others who are carrying similar responsibilities. There will be coffee and pastries, and there is no cost other than your time.

This is not a networking event (although we hope you do meet some new people), and it's not a place for sales or marketing. We hope you'll come and learn about what God says about your work. It's good news.

Looking ahead

This will be our first meeting. We plan to gather once a quarter, and you're welcome to invite other business leaders you know who might benefit from the conversation.
